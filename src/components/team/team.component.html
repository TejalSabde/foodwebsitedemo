Meet the team

Pritish Dharurkar

Perpetual Vision LLP

After completing his engineering studies and working at Decathlon, established Shawarmaji in early 2019, starting with a food truck.

His passion for food and hospitality was fueled by his extensive travels and experiences. Pritish's nuanced understanding of flavors and textures, combined with his cultural experiences and love for food, makes Shawarmaji whole.

Amey Dhadve

Perpetual Vision LLP

After completing his studies in Engineering, he has an experience in procurement. Amey embarked on his hospitality journey in 2019, starting with a food truck.

An avid traveler and fervent foodie, he has always been inspired by the hospitality industry, growing up surrounded by its impact. Amey believes in creating an unmatched experience by maintaining the highest standards of quality, hygiene, service, and customer satisfaction.

Rajas Sabde

Perpetual Vision LLP

Rajas, with an MBA in Marketing and four years of managerial experience at Decathlon, joined Shawarmaji in 2019.

Always a foodie, his extroverted nature, welcoming personality, and attention to detail epitomize the heart and soul of the brand. Rajas has brought a wealth of experience and passion to the business since joining.
