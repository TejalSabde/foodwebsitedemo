About Us

  • Shawarmaji, a quick-service restaurant serving Indo-Lebanese cuisine.
  • The model delivers high quality, freshly made, and value for money to the Lebanese food through 28 outlets operational in Thane, Mumbai, Pune and Navi Mumbai.
  • We serve unique Lebanese shawarmas with attractive branding, fresh flavors, convenient packaging and value price points.
  • PERPETUAL VISION LLP group Thane has a licensed trademark of Shawarmaji. Online presence and collaborations with Swiggy, Zomato. helps Shawarmaji to enter a new business vertical, which will lead to an increase in revenues and profitability
